[[Lios Bearna]] is an abandoned town about 15 miles south of [[Caer Ciarán]] on the road to [[Baile Coinin]].

On the Road the party may have a battle encounter

Roll a D20- On a 17-20 an encounter takes place.

[[Stirge|Stirges]]  These flying predators drain the blood of their victims and are drawn to the light of campfires at night.

[[Ghoul|Ghouls]] These undead humans hunger for living flesh.

[[Ogre]] The ogre is looking for an easy kill. It is too stupid to flee once combat is joined.

[[Goblin|Goblins]] The goblins know the location of Cragmaw Castle and can provide directions if they are captured and threatened. Each one carries a pouch containing 1d10 cp.

[[Hobgoblin|Hobgoblins]] This squad of hobgoblins is actively seeking the adventurers, hoping to collect on a bounty. They know the location of Cragmaw Castle but won’t provide directions unless they are [charmed](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#CharmedCondition). One hobgoblin carries a crudely drawn sketch of one party member, with “25 gold pieces for this one” and a symbol of a black spider drawn beneath it.

[Orcs](https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/16972-orc). These scouts are part of the band currently based at [[Baile Coinin]]. As they roam, they look for travelers to ambush or homesteads to burn.

[[Owlbear]] This hungry predator picks up the characters’ scent and pursues them relentlessly.

The Town of [[Lios Bearna]] was sacked by bandits years ago and now lies in ruins.
