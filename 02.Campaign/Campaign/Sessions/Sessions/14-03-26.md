## Events
Four adventures, [[Dian Evenflame]], [[Gruknar | His Dread Excellency Gruknar, Earl of Bumford]], [[Bruno]] and [[Grok]] are on the second day of a journey from the town of [[Deepwater]] to [[Caer Ciarán]]. They have been hired by [[Gundar Cnocín]] to escort a wagon full of mining supplies and provisions. Gundar is secretive about his reasons for this trip and the reason for the mining equipment, but he cant help but hide that he and his brothers have found "something big!"
With the promise of 10gp apiece, the adventures set out on the Western Road, following Gundar, and [[Sildar Hallwinter]], who ventured out the day before.
Having spent an uneventful first day on the road, the group set out early, aiming to make Caer Ciarán that afternoon. About two hour into their morning journey, around a bend in the road on the northern edge of Coill Deargadh the group come across a two dead horses in the road, pierced with black feathered arrows. A [[Thom | young dragonborn ]] is kneeling, offering up a prayer for the great beasts before her. As the group approaches, it is clear that these where the horses Gundar and Sildar set out on ahead of the wagon. Upon searching the area they come across an empty leather map case and a tracks that would indicate two bodies where dragged along a trail path leading into the thickets and woods. Thom, immediately drawn to the homunculus Grok, rapidly takes a place upon his shoulders.

Suddenly an arrow lances out from the thicket and glances off the shoulder pauldron of Dian, as two goblins dash from the undergrowth scimitars raised while two more remain in the undergrowth with bows and arrows.
