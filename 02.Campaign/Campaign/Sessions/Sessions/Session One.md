## Events

#### The Road to Caer Ciarán

Four adventures, Dian Evenflame, His Dread Excellency Gruknar, Earl of Bumford, Bruno and Grok are on the second day of a journey from the town of Deepwater to Caer Ciarán. They have been hired by Gundar Cnocín to escort a wagon full of mining supplies and provisions. Gundar is secretive about his reasons for this trip and the reason for the mining equipment, but he can't help but hide that he and his brothers have found "something big!"  
With the promise of 10gp apiece, the adventures set out on the Western Road, following Gundar, and Sildar Hallwinter, who ventured out the day before.  
Having spent an uneventful first day on the road, the group set out early, aiming to make Caer Ciarán that afternoon. About two hours into their morning journey, around a bend in the road on the northern edge of Coill Deargadh the group come across  two dead horses in the road, pierced with black feathered arrows. A young dragonborn is kneeling, offering up a prayer for the great beasts before her. As the group approaches, it is clear that these were the horses Gundar and Sildar set out ahead of the wagon. Upon searching the area they come across an empty leather map case and a track that would indicate two bodies were dragged along a trail path leading into the thickets and woods. Thom, immediately drawn to the homunculus Grok, rapidly takes a place upon his shoulders.

#### Goblin Ambush

Suddenly an arrow lances out from the thicket and glances off the shoulder pauldron of Dian, as two goblins dash from the undergrowth scimitars raised while two more remain in the undergrowth with bows and arrows. Grok, enraged by the Goblin ambush, rushes forward and unwieldy spanks a goblin with the flat of his great axe. Upon his shoulders Thom spits acid in the face of the Goblin.  
Bruno and Dian quickly make short work of the two goblins armed with scimitars, while Gruknar fires off an Eldritch Blast destroying one of the goblins firing arrows. The final goblin, realizing that this is a doomed ambush, attempts to flee, but is quickly dispatched by the adventurers.

#### The Goblin trail

The adventurers take time to decide the best course of action. They would look to carry on to Caer Ciaran, but knowing that their Patron Gundar is unlikely to be able to pay them for delivering his wagon if he has been captured by Goblins, decide to move the Wagon off the Road into a clearing by the tree line and follow the path alongside the stream into the woods. As they move along Thom, still sitting atop Groks shoulders, spots at the last minute a trip-wire, a booby-trap left by the goblins on the entrance to their hideout.  
In a clearing at the end of the trail, the party see the stream they have been following emerge from the mouth of a cave. They will have to cross the shallow stream to enter the cave. Grok with Thom still aboard makes the first move, as they get to the other side, an arrow shot from behind them in a small clearing in the thickets strikes Thom. The party springs into action and again makes light work of the two goblins hiding in watch. Grok lifts a goblin, unhinges his jaw and begins to swallow the goblin whole.

#### Into the Caves

Just inside the entrance to the caves the party passes an entrance up some small uneven stone steps. Peering into the cave with the light from outside, the party sees three wolves chained. Thom is immediately frightened of the sight of these large dogs attempting to scramble from Groks shoulder. Grok wades into the Wolf Den and attempts to bargain with the wolves by regurgitating the half eaten goblin. Unpleased with the offering the wolves instead, strain at their chains and take chunks out of Grok's arms and Torso, before the final wolf makes a lunge for the throat, bring the titan Grok to his knees as the arcane forces that gave him life slipped away.

Keeping their distance from the wolves the rest of the party discuss their options. Gruknar, having spent time in some goblin caves before offers some information to the party. These caves usually have a main passageway that is probably guarded or being watched by a sentry. If they want to pass unobserved they will have to do so without light. As the party make their way up the cave, being led by the Orc Gruknar who has excellent vision in the dark, ahead he spots a bridge passing above the pathway. Silently the party moves under the bridge, avoiding the eyes of anyone who might be keeping watch. They continue to climb the path around a bend, there is a cave on their left where the source of the stream appears to be coming from, and a continuation of the path to the right towards the bridge they saw earlier. Making for the bridge, the party sees a lazy goblin sentry on the far side of the bridge, not keeping watch at all. Bruno readies a javelin to throw, while Gruknar fires off a streak of Dark Energy, taking the Goblin completely by surprise as he is killed instantly.

#### The Goblin Den

Continuing to sneak along the path the party again comes to a cavern entrance. Stealthily the creep to the threshold, and spy within six goblins around some campfires with a half-elven human man, badly injured and tied to a chair, this is Sildar who all but Thom had met 2 days previous with Gundar in Deepwater.

Thinking quickly the party gets the drop on the goblins rushing in the party very quickly dispatch two of the goblins. Making use of the distraction Sildar stands from the chair, unable to break the bonds that tie him, but able to snap the flimsy wooden chair he had been tied to. The biggest goblin trades blows with Dian and Bruno. But seeing the battle turning against him as a third of his goblins are killed, attempts to surrender, offering a truce and the promise of riches and information, in exchange for the head of Klarg, the Bugbear leader of the Cragmaw Cave Goblins.

Yeemik, the Goblin Chief, spouts half sentences wishing to take over from Klarg, and a fearful rant about "The Black Spider". From what the party can gather, the Black Spider, whoever it or they are, told the Goblins to ambush the Dwarf and deliver him.  
Sildar suggests not trusting the Goblin, this gang has been ambushing wagons on the road for a long time. Needing no second opinion Dian raised his battleaxe and swiftly separated Yeemik's head from his shoulders. While Gruknar and Bruno and Thom dispatch the remaining two goblins.

Sildar thanks the party for the rescue and informs them that Gundar, and his brothers Tharden and Nundro had discovered what they believe to be a map to the Echoing Hollows, a legendary and lost to time and myth, mine where the God Riarann the Smith once pulled Iarnchroi from the earth and with it forged the First Plough and the first Sword.  
Sildar is unaware of who the Black Spider that Yeemik referred to, but he believes that Gundar was sent by Klarg, along with his map to Cragmaw Castle, further inside Coill Deargadh, he isn't sure of the route to the castle, but someone in Caer Ciarán might be more familiar of the woods and notable locations within.  
Sildar intends to carry on to Caer Ciarán but first he asks the adventurers for assistance in riding Cragmaw Cave of its goblin problem and their ambushes on the road.  
Sildar is provided with a Potion of Healing by one of the Gruknar (or lay on hands by Bruno??) and restores some of his Vitality.

##### The Twin Pools Cave and Klarg

Moving back through the cave system, the party and Sildar come across the other part of the cave system not explored before crossing the bridge.  
In this cavern the party sees two Large Pools which have been partially dammed, feeding into the stream. Within are three more goblins. Again with ruthless precision the goblins are quickly dispatched before alarm or defense can be raised.

Down some stone steps the party spot another cavern with a large bonfire burning brightly. Along the back wall of the cavern are stacked many crates, chests and assorted goods, the spoils of the Goblin Ambushes. Around the fire are two goblins, a large Bugbear; Klarg, and a wolf. Not having the element of surprise this time the party and the Goblins join battle. Dian goes straight for Klarg striking him with his battleaxe. Klarg responds in kind with his Morningstar injuring Gruknar. Thom spits acid at one of the goblins. Gruknar casts a spell on Klarg whose wounds from Dian's initial assault appear to become infected with a deep black ichor. Ripper, Klarg's pet wolf makes a leap for Gruknar clamping his jaws around Gruknars shoulder. Feeling the strength leaving his body, Gruknar begins to fall under the weight of the wolf. With one last effort he steadied himself, dug deep into his own strength as an Orc and fought to remain conscious, pushing the wolf from off him.  
Sildar circled around the back of Klarg, harrying him on two fronts as he attempted to fight Dian. Distracted, Dian was able to get the jump on the injured Klarg and with a swift blow struck through the neck. The black ichor from Gruknar's magic causes the body to rapidly deteriorate.  
Sildar took his longsword and thrust it through the gaping maw of the wolf leaping for another attack. Dead, the wolf fell onto the pyre. The final goblins were quickly dispatched having seen their leader disposed of.

Within the cavern the group finds a treasure chest which contains 600cp, 110sp, (17gp equivalent), two potions of healing and a small Jade Frog Statuette with 2 golden eyes.

Searching the goblins' ill-gotten spoils. The party found 3 crates bearing the Symbol of Lionshead Coster, a supply company, with delivery information for their outpost in Caer Ciarán. Seeing an opportunity for an extra payday, the party take the crates with them, stacking them on their wagon and continuing on to Caer Ciarán.

#### Caer Ciarán

Arriving in Caer Ciaran, Linene, the Owner of the Caer Ciarán branch of Lionshead Coster pay the party 50gp for the safe delivery of her goods. She tells the party she is in their debt and will offer what help she can if they return after having rested.

Continuing on to Barthen's Goods. Barthen takes delivery of Gundar's stock. He pays the party what Gundar has promised (10gp each) and says to return tomorrow after they have had a chance to refresh themselves after their long and arduous journey.

Finally, Sildar also provided the party with a leather coin purse containing 50gp for rescuing him. He then departed saying he had some business to attend to, but he would reconvene with them to discuss what must be done next.  
The party retire to the tavern, purchase food and drink for the evening and retire to their rented rooms.

## Key Learnings 

- Black Spider
	- Who is the Black Spider?
    
- Echoing Hollows
    - An almost mythological site, if real it could hold huge riches for whoever controls it.
- Iarnchroi
	- A legendary metal, it is believed some of the most magical and oldest weapons are made of this rare metal.
- Gundar's location
	- The party believes that Gundar has been moved to Cragmaw Castle, but they may learn more information in Caer Ciarán about where that is.
