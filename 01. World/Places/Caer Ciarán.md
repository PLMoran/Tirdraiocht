Caer Ciarán is a frontier town in the foothills of [[Sliabh Liath]]. It lies approximately 50 miles west of [[Deepwater]].
#### Important NPCS
**Toblen Stonehill**: Innkeeper

**Elmar Barthen**: Owns a trading post; owes money to the party on delivery of [[Gundar Cnocín| Gundar's]] Wagon

**Daran Edermath**: Member of the Order of the Gauntlet with a quest for the party

**Linene Graywind**: Runs a trading post and offers a reward for retrieving her supplies

**Halia Thornton**: Member of the Zhentarim with a quest for the party

**Qelline Alderleaf**: Helpful halfling farmer whose son, Carp, knows a secret way into the Redbrands’ hideout

**Sister Garaele**: Elf cleric and Harper agent with a quest for the party

**Harbin Wester**: Townmaster of [[Caer Ciarán]] with a quest for the party

[[Sildar Hallwinter]]: Member of the [[Mic na Seachtar]] with two quests for the party
