#### The Imperial Region
**Map**
The Empire covers the heart of the land of Tírdraiocht between Sliabh Liath on the Western Peninsula to Sléibhte na nDéithe. It spans about 400 miles East to West and 600 miles North to south.

##### Capital City: Place Holder


##### Deepwater
Deepwater is a city on the Northern shore of the Long Lough. It provides a strategic stronghold by controlling the deep waters which have allowed the Empire to grow its Navy, protecting the settlements on the Eastern Shore, and exerting their influence on the contested frontier of the Empire in the Western Peninsula.
It also acts as a land checkpoint on the narrow neck connecting the Western Peninsula to the rest of the continent. A vital city in the Empire, its port, provides military and economic strength.
