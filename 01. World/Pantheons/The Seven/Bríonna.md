### Goddess of Birth, Fertility and the First Breath

##### Domains:
Birth and Pregnancy
Fertility of Land, Beast and the Peoples
Renewal and Spring

##### Symbol:
Twisted Reed Knot
![[Brionna's Knot]]This Symbol is hung above cradles, barn doors and seed stores.

##### Worship
Bríonna is worshipped especially by Agricultural Workers, Mothers, and Healers.

##### Personality
Bríonna is tender but unyielding.
She loves easily, but she is not sentimental, death is as much a part of life, flowers, crops, animals, children, all must wither, but life springs renewed from death.
