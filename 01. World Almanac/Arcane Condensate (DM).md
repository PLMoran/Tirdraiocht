# Arcane Condensate
_Rare Consumable (Drug), Magical_

![[Pasted image 20260519203733.png]]
> [!danger] **GM Eyes Only** 
> This version contains addiction mechanics, comedown effects, corruption, and hidden consequences not shown to players.

## **Primary Effect (1 Dose)**

For **1 hour**:
- Gain **1 temporary spell slot** of a level **one higher** than the user’s highest spell slot
- Non‑casters gain **one 1st‑level slot** and may cast a scroll spell without consuming the scroll

## **Stacking Doses (Within 24 Hours)**

|Dose|Mechanical Effect|
|---|---|
|**2nd Dose**|+1 boosted slot **or** a slot **two levels higher** (max 6th)|
|**3rd Dose**|+1 slot **one level higher** than previous (max 8th)|
|**4th+ Dose**|Each dose triggers **Overdose Save**|

### **Overdose Save**

**Constitution Save DC = 15 + (doses beyond 3)** On failure:

- **1 level of exhaustion**
- **Wild Magic Surge** (even if not a sorcerer)
- **Hit point maximum reduced by 1d6** until next long rest

## **Comedown Effects**

After the 1‑hour duration ends:

- **Arcane Fatigue:** Disadvantage on concentration checks for 1 hour
- **Spell Burn:** Lose **one spell slot of the highest level available**
- **Arcane Tremors:** First spell cast has a **25% chance to fizzle** (slot lost)

## **Addiction Mechanics**

After each long rest following a day of use:

### **Addiction Save**

**Wisdom Save DC 10**, increasing by **+2** for each consecutive day of use A week without using resets DC to 10

### **On a Failed Save — Craving (24 hours)**

At the start of combat:

- Make a **Wisdom save (DC 12)**
    - **Success:** May choose whether to use Condensate
    - **Failure:** Must use a dose if available

If no dose is available:
- Disadvantage on spell attack rolls
- Spell save DC −1
- Disadvantage on concentration checks

## **Withdrawal (If Addicted and User Stops)**

Lasts **1d4+1 days**:

- Disadvantage on Arcana checks
- Spell save DC −1
- Cannot benefit from Condensate
- Roll 1d6 for magical symptoms

|d6|Withdrawal Symptom|
|---|---|
|1|Hands spark; disadvantage on Sleight of Hand|
|2|Eyes glow; disadvantage on Stealth|
|3|Spells create unintended illusions|
|4|Telekinetic bursts under stress|
|5|Arcane nightmares; no benefit from first long rest|
|6|Wild Magic Surge on first spell cast each day|

## **Optional: Arcane Corruption Track**

Gain **1 Corruption Point** per dose.

|Points|Effect|
|---|---|
|**5**|Veins glow; disadvantage on Deception|
|**10**|Sorcerous mutation (GM chooses or roll on Wild Magic table)|
|**15**|Permanently: +1 spell attack & DC, −1 Wisdom|
|**20**|Character becomes a **Wild Magic Sorcerer**|
