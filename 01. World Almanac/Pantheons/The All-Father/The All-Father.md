The Faith of the All-Father is a Monotheistic belief, predominantly followed within the Empire.
It is the Official Faith of the Astantau Empire, and while celebration of the Seven is not illegal, Suppression of the Old Gods is becoming more and more common especially within the Imperial Core.

The All-Father is believed to be Creator, he flattened the Land, Raised the Mountains, Carved the Oceans and Sowed the Seed.

Aspects of the Seven have been subsumed within the faith of the All-Father, in an attempt by the Temple of the All-Father to bridge the gap between those that hold faith in the Old-Gods.
