5 hours north of [[Caer Ciarán]]

Orcs in the area have been causing trouble for famers and homesteaders in the area. Some citizens of Caer Ciarán will ask the party to deal with the orc problem.

The orcs can be fought and killed/driven from the area.

A succesful perception check (DC15) if approaching with caution will reveal that there appear to be families of Orcs living in and around the tower, including elderly, and children.

If friendly contact is initiated with the orcs and a successful persuasion check (advantage if Gruknar makes the attempt) will lead to a conversation about how the Orc's are not barbarians, they have been forced into a fight for survival from Imperial settlers claiming land that once belonged to the Orcs. They have lost their land, their crops and their homes, and are expected to uproot and leave the land they cultivated for generations.

Options:
Party Convinces the Orcs to leave the land and the settlers, and attempt to travel to new lands. (best  case scenario for survival of the orcs)

Party allows the orcs to remain, with the warning that more attacks will bring the imperial military down on them. 

If combat ensues, and the warriors are destroyed (6 orcs) then the rest of the settlement will agree to move north and attempt top find safe passage around the mountains. (Travelling north at a later date, the party will encounter the destroyed remains of the orc group close to the northern pass)
