The Mountain of the Gods
A mountain range that runs from North to South of the Continent of Tírdraiocht.

Beanna bhFlaitheas, is the predominant mountain in the range. 

Aelthirs Summit is the seat of the Aelthir, alongside him sits Osgaire, God of dreams.
Together the oversee both the visible and invisible realms of sovereignty.
Alongisde, them Nemainne spins fate from flax grown in Brionna's fields

Deep in the heart of the mountain lies the Forge of Riarann. Here in the fires Riarann crafted tools and weapons for his fellow gods, and the first children.The forge is quenced by Abhann Mór, the Great River that springs from the heart of the mountain.

Abhann Mór flows out through the land towards the ocean, Muirath swims the waters from source to sea. 

In the foothills of the mountain the Goddesses live, Cathmórra taught the First Children to fight against the corrupting creatures of the world.
Bríonna tends to the land and its people, growing flowers for beauty, crops and cattle for sustenance and guiding the growth and wellbeing of the peoples of Tírdraiocht.
Nemainne, spins the fates from flax grown in Brionna's fields, her great loom weaving the tapestry of time.
