Deepwater is a port town at the north of the long lough. Its deep water allows for a strong ship building industry, and a seat of the empires military and merchant navys to set out from. protecting the eastern coast of the lough in the Empires heartland, and excerting influence over the western shores, in the empires contested frontier. It also provides access out to the great ocean.

#### The Imperial Naval Yards
- A shipwright goes missing after discovering sabotage.
    
- A new warship is cursed — sailors hear voices from the hull.
    
- A stolen prototype weapon must be recovered before it leaves the city.
    
- A press‑gang mistakenly (or deliberately) conscripts an NPC ally.

#### The Admiralty
Where admirals, naval priests of the Seven Pillars, and imperial bureaucrats reside.

**Adventure hooks:**

- A high-ranking official hires the party for a discreet investigation.
    
- A priest claims prophetic dreams from Osgaire about the western frontier.
    
- A rival faction within the navy seeks to undermine the current admiral.
    
- A sealed archive contains forbidden lore about the old gods and the ocean.
    

This is where the Empire’s polished façade cracks.

#### The Western Gate
Deepwater's position on the narrow neck of land connecting the Western Peninsula to the rest of the continent, means it is also as strategic defensive fortification on land as it is its connection to the sea. Everything beyond the Western Gate to [[Sliabh Liath]], although claimed by the Empire, is contested. Bandits and Wild Things roam and harry travellers. And Those who oppose the Empire, its expansion and the imposition of their Priests find themselves on the outside.
