Old Owl Well is a ruined tower to the south west of [[Caer Ciarán]]

A fresh water well sits in the middle of the ruined tower. It is frequented at night by screech Owls from where the tower gets it  name.

Recently Hamun Kost, a necromancer has taken up residence at the tower.
He along with his Zombie thralls have been causing a stirring amongst the prospectors of the area.
