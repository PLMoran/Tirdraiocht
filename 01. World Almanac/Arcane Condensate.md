# Arcane Condensate 
_Rare Consumable (Drug), Magical_
![[Pasted image 20260519203745.png]]
> [!info] **What It Is** 
> A pouch of powdered arcane essence. Consuming it floods your body with raw magical potential.

## **Effects (1 Dose)**

For **1 hour**, you gain:
- **1 temporary spell slot** of a level **one higher** than your highest spell slot
- If you have no spell slots, you gain **one 1st‑level slot** and may cast a spell from a scroll without consuming it

## **Multiple Doses (Within 24 Hours)**

|Dose|Effect|
|---|---|
|**2nd Dose**|Gain an additional boosted slot **or** a slot **two levels higher** (max 6th)|
|**3rd+ Dose**|Gain a temporary slot **one level higher than the last dose’s slot** (max 8th)|

## **Immediate Drawbacks**

After the effect ends:

- **Arcane Fatigue:** Disadvantage on concentration checks for 1 hour
    
- **Spell Burn:** Lose **one spell slot of your highest level** (minimum 1st)
    
- **Arcane Tremors:** Your next spell has a **25% chance to fizzle** (slot still spent)
    

> [!warning] **Risk of Overuse** 
> Taking more than **3 doses in 24 hours** risks dangerous magical overload.
