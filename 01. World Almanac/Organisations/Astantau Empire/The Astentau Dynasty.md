Year 120
Queen Lirenne Ascends to the throne at age 70.

Year 121
King Brannoc II becomes until now the youngest king at age 32. 
He rules for 29 years 

Year 150
King Cadan II, the Eldest Son of Brannoc II becomes king
He died tragically in a hunting expedition after less than a year as King aged 47 and childless

Year 151
King Torren the Pious, the Brother of Cadan II is Crowned
Torren being the second son of the King never expected to take the crown, and from a young age he had spent his time in study and prayer.
He was a devout adherent of the All-Father, which from Ulraf united the Human lords was always the religion of the Astantau family and the Kingdom, until then lived Harmoniously with the belief of the Seven. Torren began the suppression of the Seven within the Empire.
Torren set about the great task of resetting the Calendar. He set the first year as the year Ulraf The Great ascended to the throne.
King Torren ruled to age 95

Year 337
**Queen Muriel** - 18 years
A queen Devoted to the worship of the All-Father. She had a vision soon after coming to her throne that her as yet to be born son would be the greatest King to rule the empire. She was convinced that he was to be the great Uniter, the All-Father incarnate, to bring his rule over all the lands. She Abdicated her throne when Ulraf was just 17 years old, making him the youngest King to ever take the Throne.

Year 353

The Young King has been raised at his mother knee to believe he will bring peace to all the lands of the continent. expanding and securing the Empires borders from the Shining Coast to the lands beyond Sleibhte na nDeithe!
