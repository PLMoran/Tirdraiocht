The Weavers, a scattered network of adventurers and spies who advocate equality and covertly oppose the abuse of power. The Weavers gather information throughout the land to thwart tyrants and any leader, government, or group that grows too strong. They aid the weak, the poor, and the oppressed. They are guided by their patron Goddess Nemainne, who dooms the fate of all, the great and the lowly.
